GLN Final Project Rough Plan

Type of Game: 1v1 deck-based constructive card game with tile/board elements

Description/Rules: The game is played with two players, on a 10 x 10 board. In the top two corners of the board are the players bases (A and B, one for each player). In the bottom left corner of the border there is the electricity start, and in the bottom right corner the water start. The goal of the game is to connect both electricity and water to your base. Each player starts with a deck of some cards  and a large pile of at least 150-200 tiles are placed near the board. Players alternate taking turns. A turn is composed of drawing a player drawing a card from the deck of that player, drawing a tile randomly from the communal pile, playing a card, and then playing a tile. A player has to play at least a card or a tile each turn. A player begins the game with 5 random cards and 5 random tiles.

Here are some example cards (NOTE: All card effects override previously stated rules regarding the drawing of cards and tiles and the playing of cards and tiles, however the rules going towards the placement of tiles may not be overridden at any time):

·         Play two tiles this turn instead of one

·         Choose any tile on the board and replace it with a tile from your hand

·         Rotate any tile on the board

·         Remove any tile on the board, but don’t play a tile this turn

·         Skip this turn and draw and play two tiles next turn

·         Re-roll a tile effect (Some tiles have an aspect of randomness, such as electrical switch tiles, and these tiles have a specified amount of dice rolled to determine something about the tile)

·         Choose a tile to give your opponent, and they have to choose a tile to give you

·         AOE Card (AOE cards effect all play for entire players until they are destroyed, either by a specific card or another AOE card): Hand limit of three for both tiles and cards, discard down to this amount if you have more

·         Destroy the current AOE card

·         AOE Card: Players draw two cards and two tiles each turn

·         Choose one card randomly from your opponents hand, and put that card into your hand

Specific Tile Placement Rules:

·         Tiles have pipes and wires on them; each end to a pipe or wire has a color/number on it (not decided which it is yet) and pipes have to connect with pipes of the same color/number. Wires also have to connect with wires of the same/color number

·         Tiles can only be played if all the pipes/wires on that tile connect correctly using the previous rules
